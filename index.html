About Space Invaders

Created by: Roni Gorlizki, Hadar Azaria

Game Instructions:

  • Use arrow keys to move your spaceship
  • Press the configured key to shoot
  • Destroy all enemy ships to win
  • Avoid enemy missiles - you have 3 lives
  • Points: Brown enemies (top row) = 20 points
  • Points: Red enemies (second row) = 15 points
  • Points: Orange enemies (third row) = 10 points
  • Points: Yellow enemies (bottom row) = 5 points
  • Enemy ships accelerate every 5 seconds!
